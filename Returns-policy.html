Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Here's everything you need to know about our returns policy.

憋

Return Window

You have 14 calendar days from the date of purchase to initiate a return.

Condition of Items

Items must be returned in their original condition, unworn, unwashed, and with all tags attached.

Refund Method

Refunds are processed back to the original payment method used for the purchase. The refund can take up to 10 days after receipt of the item.

Final Sale Items

Items that are marked as final sale are not eligible for return.

Important Note: Return shipping costs are covered by the customer.

Attention: Exchanges are not allowed. If you purchased the wrong size or color, please return the item and make a new purchase.

Return Process

1

Initiate Your Request

Contact us via our contact form on our website. Include your order number and reason for return.

2

Receive Instructions

We will email you detailed instructions for returning the item, including return shipping details.

3

Pack and Ship

Ensure items are in their original condition with all tags attached. Use secure packaging and ship to the address provided.

4

Processing and Refund

Once your return is received and verified, we will process your refund to your original payment method within 10 days.

Frequently Asked Questions about Returns

Can I exchange my item for a different size or color?

No, we do not offer direct exchanges. You will need to return your item and make a new purchase for the desired size or color.

How can I track the status of my refund?

Once we have received and processed your return, you will receive a confirmation email. If you have questions regarding the status of your refund, please contact us through our form on the FAQ page.

What happens if my item arrives damaged?

If you receive a damaged item, please contact us immediately via our contact form with photos of the item and packaging. We will prioritize your request.

Need Help with Your Return?

Our customer service team is here to assist you with any questions regarding returns.
